About Me

Hi, I’m Dominic — an online tutor helping Grade 5–8 learners grow their confidence in computing and related subjects. I believe growth requires change, and I enjoy exploring new ideas, coding, and learning alongside my students.

Teaching

I focus on making complex ideas simple, hands-on, and engaging. My lessons often include programming basics, Scratch, HTML/CSS/JS, and practical problem-solving.

Adventures

I plan to hike, camp, bungee jump, and become a strong swimmer. I dream of exploring Mt. Kenya, Turkana, the Maasai Mara, and even trying skydiving one day.

Philosophy

I enjoy exploring ethics — especially a blend of objectivism and subjectivism. Kantian ethics makes sense to me as a foundation, balanced with lived experience.
